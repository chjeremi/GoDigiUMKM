Frequently Asked Questions about AI & MSME Digitalization

Find answers to the most common questions about how our AI technology can help grow your MSME.

How does AI Gemini help MSMEs make better decisions?
AI Gemini processes sales data, customer behavior, market trends, and other external data. From this analysis, Gemini provides **predictive insights**, such as which products will be popular, when is the best time for promotions, or how to optimize prices, so MSMEs can make **efficient, effective, and data-driven decisions** without needing deep data science expertise.
Is my business data safe when used by the AI?
Data security is our top priority. All data is encrypted to the highest standards and processed according to strict **privacy policies**. AI Gemini only uses the data to provide relevant insights and recommendations for your business, not for other purposes.
Do I need technical or data science expertise to use the AI features?
Absolutely not! Our AI features are designed to be **very intuitive and easy to use**. You will receive recommendations and analysis in an easy-to-understand language through a user-friendly dashboard, without needing a technical or data science background.
How can UMKM Digital be integrated with my current system?
Our platform is designed with **flexible APIs** for integration with various common business systems (e.g., POS or accounting systems). Our technical team is also ready to provide guidance and assistance for a seamless integration process.
